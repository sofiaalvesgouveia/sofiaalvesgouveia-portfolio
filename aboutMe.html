Born and raised in Porto, I'm a portuguese communication designer. I first discovered my love for arts and design back in high school at Escola Artística Soares dos Reis, and later refined my Communication Design skills at Faculdade de Belas Artes da Universidade do Porto.

My main areas of interest include Editorial Design, Photography, Branding, and Web Design. Outside of design, I find inspiration (and distraction) in many places: knitting and crochet, sudoku puzzles and books that I always start but almost never finish.

When I’m not experimenting with grids, typefaces, or color palettes, you’ll most likely find me happily distracted by cats (yes, I truly believe every great designer should have at least one feline muse). If creativity runs on coffee, mine runs on purring and the occasional cat hair on the keyboard.
